Ingredients:
 - 2 ground turkey
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 2 jalepeno peppers, diced
 - 1 tablespoons cayenne chili powder
 - 3 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon ancho chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 pound grated cheese
 - 1/2 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
 - 2 medium size tomatos, diced
 - 1/2 yellow onion, diced
 - 1 jar of you favorite salsa
 - 1 container pre-made guacamole
 - 1 package of corn or flower tortillas

Directions:
 1. Brown the ground turkey in a large pan and drain off the excess fat.
 1a. Turn heat to low and add garlic and jalepeno peppers . Let simmer 3 minutes.
 2. Add chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and cayenne to a small bowl, add a cup of water.
 3. Turn heat to medium high and add mixture while stirring. When it bubbles, turn to low and cover. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
 4. Separate remaining ingredients to individual bowls with their own serving spoon.
 5. Warm tortillas
 6. Add one scoop of Spicy Turkey Taco meat to each tortilla and serve.
 6a. Add condiments to taste.
 7. Fold over and ENJOY!
